Multi-Modal Input

Direct Audio & Whiteboard OCR

No text transcript? No problem. Kall AI leverages Gemini's native multi-hour audio window to transcribe and summarize directly from raw audio files (.mp3, .wav, .m4a). Combine it with whiteboard sketches or slide deck screenshots, and the multi-modal LLM automatically extracts architectural decisions and drawings discussed in the room.

  • No third-party transcription: Native multi-hour audio processing via Gemini API.

Output that never breaks

HOW IT WORKS

Before, during, and after the meeting.

🔑

Set up: add your API key in 30 seconds

Open Advanced Settings, pick Google Gemini (audio + text) or Anthropic Claude (text), and paste your own API key. Keys are securely stored in your profile. No key? Use the built-in demo transcript.

📝

Drop your meeting

Paste a transcript, upload .txt/.srt/.vtt/.pdf, upload audio (.mp3/.wav/.m4a — Gemini), or record your screen + audio in-browser. Pick a meeting template (1:1, Sprint, Client Discovery, Retro) to tune extraction.

🚀

Extract → 5 result views

One click returns an executive brief, an Agile action backlog, speaker talk-share analytics, an Ask-PM Copilot Q&A, and an optional one-click Jira or Linear ticket sync.

⚡ App Fast-Track Checklist

  • Pick engine + paste API key : Open the 🛠️ Advanced Settings expander on the left. Choose Google Gemini (audio + text) or Anthropic Claude (text only) and paste your own API key. Keys are securely stored in your profile.
  • Enter your name : Personalize the workspace under 👤 Who's running this session? at the top of the left panel.
  • Pick a meeting template : General Sync, 1:1 Feedback, Sprint Planning, Client Discovery, or Retrospective — tailors the extraction format.
  • Drop a transcript : Use the Paste text, Upload file, or Audio chips on the right. No transcript? Click 💡 Try with demo transcript to auto-load the built-in Q3 sprint sample.
  • Tick the consent box : Required for compliance with local wiretapping laws.
  • Extract insights : Click 🚀 Extract Operational Insights to run the extraction engine.
  • Explore 5 views : Executive Dashboard, Agile Action Planner, Speaker Analytics, Ask PM Copilot, and Issue Sync tabs appear after first extraction.
  • Export or sync : Click 📑 Export PDF in the top-right header, or open the Issue Sync tab to push action items as Jira or Linear tickets in one click.
